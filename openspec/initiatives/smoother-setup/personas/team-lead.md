# Persona: Lead coordinating several repos

## Who they are

Someone who owns a goal that spans more than one repo, or more than one person.

## What they want

One shared plan everyone can see, and a clear view of what is done and what is
left.

## What gets in their way

Plans copied into each repo, slowly going out of date. No single source of
truth.

## How this initiative helps

The initiative groups the related changes in one place. A store shares that plan
across repos, so every project reads the same source instead of a copy.
